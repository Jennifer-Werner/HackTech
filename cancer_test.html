Breast Cancer Assessment

Our assessment is based on cell nuclei characterstics that could be received through mammograms. The assessment could help to evaluate the data and make predictions on diagnosis with 95% accuracy.

Mammogram Data Interpretation

We are developing a neural network for direct intepretation of data and prediction of breast cancer risks from mammograph screening. You no longer need to input data manually and can receive your results quickly.

Cancer Screening Device

We are working on a device for monitoring breast cancer related parameters, developing more affordable and accessible solutions for assessment. Data from the device can be syncronized with the application alongside with other data.
